From Hobby to Mission

ToyGun Vault started as a small passion project in 2018 when our founder, a lifelong airsoft enthusiast, realized there was no dedicated marketplace for high-quality toy guns and replicas that treated the hobby with the respect it deserved.

What began as a garage operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in the toy firearm community. We curate every product in our catalog personally, ensuring that each item meets our strict standards for quality, safety, and fun.

Today, we serve over 15,000 customers worldwide, from competitive airsoft players and Nerf warriors to movie prop collectors and cosplayers. Our mission remains the same: to provide the best toy gun experience possible.
